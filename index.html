Publication-ready biomedical figures — right in your browser

Open Bio Canvas is a free, open-source illustration studio for life scientists. Create pathway diagrams, cell illustrations, protein structures, and published-quality figures without leaving your browser.

Open Bio Canvas running in your browser

Everything you need for scientific figures

🔬

2,700+ Bio Icons

Browse cells, pathways, proteins, organs, and more. Customise colours, scale, and placement with a single click.

📊

Publication Charts

Bar, line, area, scatter, and box plots with CVD-safe palettes and journal-specific presets. Import CSV data directly.

🧬

Protein Studio

3D protein visualisation with Molstar and 3Dmol. Search PDB, render SVG cartoons, place structures on the canvas.

🎯

Precision Arrows

Activation, inhibition, catalysis, forked, and orthogonal pathway connectors with editable curvature and heads.

🎨

WebGL Glass UI

Real-time refraction, Fresnel reflections, and chromatic aberration. Choose from 5 glass presets or tune every parameter.

💾

Export Anywhere

PNG (1-4×), SVG, JSON, and Open Figure Package. Publication preflight checks for stroke width, text size, and CVD compliance.

🧬

Lipid Membranes

Parametric bilayer builder with curvature, embedded channels, receptors, and GPCRs. Realistic or flat styles.

✁

Vector Knife Tool

Split any shape with a drag. Objects stay fully editable after cutting. Freehand brush for quick annotations.

🌐

Local & Private

Works entirely in your browser. No server, no account, no data leaves your machine. Optional Google Drive sync.
